# Startup Notes

Technical and market notes from working around startup ecosystems, cloud platforms,
AI infrastructure, and applied AI systems.

I use this section to collect observations on AI infrastructure markets, startup
architecture, GPU/cloud economics, developer tooling, technical diligence, and the
practical path from prototype to production.

This is not investment advice. It is a public record of technical pattern recognition.

The notes are grounded in startup-facing cloud work, including AWS Startups context,
architecture reviews, founder/operator conversations, and public AI market analysis.
Selected AI and technology advisory work with venture teams informs some of the
questions here, but this page excludes private deal flow, founder names, investment
memos, and confidential analysis.
